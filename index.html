Aravind Srinivas

I'm a first year PhD student at UC Berkeley and part of Berkeley AI Research. Prior to this, I completed my undergrad at IIT Madras.

I'm primarily interested in Meta-Learning with applications to simulated robotic tasks, and broadly interested in developing algorithms that can enable autonomous learning with low sample complexity for high dimensional problems.

Contact me at aravind@cs.berkeley.edu.
